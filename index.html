The Problem

An estimated 12.2 crore people have lost their jobs in India due to the COVID-19 pandemic, 3/4th of which are small traders. It has become extremely challenging for small businesses to survive without shifting their business online. However, E-commerce is still a foreign concept to many street-side vendors, shop-owners and merchants.

The Solution

Rozgar provides a three way solution to simplify e-commerce and to make it accessible to everyone with the Rozgar Seller, Consumer, and Delivery apps. These three applications work in harmony to create opportunities for merchants and local businesses to take their ventures online and to provide local drivers and workers with an alternative source of income.

Seller App

The seller app is specially designed while considering the fact our "Rozgars" would be people with little to no experience doing business online. The Rozgar Seller App has an intuitive user interface and technologies to cater to those unfamiliar with E-Commerce.

Customer App

We display the products added by our Rozgars through the seller app, on the Rozgar Customer App, which is available on the Playstore and App Store. We present all products in a professional manner suited for an urban market. The customer app allows users to discover local businesses and products, and facilitates a wonderful online shopping experience.

Delivery App

The deliver app completely liberalises the product delivery process by allowing drivers/riders to set up an account and claim orders for products placed through the Consumer app at locations near them and deliver them to the required locations within 12-15 hours and earn based on number of deliveries.
